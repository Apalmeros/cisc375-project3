Tools Used

Leaflet API

Open-source JavaScript library which focuses on creating and customizing interactive maps for users. Used by numerous big-name companies, such as GitHub, Facebook, The Washington Post, and Etsy.

Nominatim

Searches data from OpenStreetMap (OSM) to convert a street address or location name into a coordinate location on a map, or translate a coordinate location into a street address or location name. These processes are known as geocoding and reverse-geocoding, respectively.

Vue.JS

Open-source JavaScript framework that is among the most popular in the world, also known for its overall simplicity. Used by numerous big-name companies, such as Nintendo, Adobe, BMW, and Facebook.

Project 3 - RESTful Server

Uses SQLite3 to search a public dataset on crime in St. Paul and displays information based on what the user is looking for (entered through the URL). Such information includes the type of crime committed, the neighborhood where it occurred, when it happened, and the approximate address of the crime.

Interesting Findings

  1. According to our map, North End is the neighborhood with the most crimes (147 out of sample of 1000) and Summit Hill is the neighborhood with the least (22 out of sample of 1000).
  2. Frogtown is obviously a funny name for a St. Paul neighborhood, and its origin dates as far back as the 1860s. Known originally as "Froschenburg" by the German-Bohemian settlers who first moved there, the area was characterized at the time by its numerous swamps and marshes (and therefore its high frog population).
  3. In terms of St. Paul as a whole, the University of St. Thomas is located in a very good area-- according to our map, the two neighborhoods it inhabits (Macalester - Groveland and Union Park) are among the least crime-heavy neighborhoods in the city, with only 29 and 34 crimes (respectively) out of our sample of 1000.
  4. According to the city bounds established by our map, the University of Minnesota's St. Paul Campus technically isn't located in St. Paul at all-- it's technically in Falcon Heights.
  5. According to our map, with the exceptions of the West End neighborhood and Pike Island in the southern Highland neighborhood, the southern (and most of the eastern) city limits of St. Paul are entirely defined by the Mississippi River.
  6. According to our map, the 3M headquarters are *almost* located in St. Paul, but are entirely found outside of the St. Paul city limits. Very sad :(
